Effective Problem Solving

November 2024

Introduction

Physics problem solving is a skill that can be developed systematically. In this post, I share strategies that have helped my students approach complex problems with confidence.

Key Strategies

  • Identify the physical principles at play
  • Draw diagrams and define coordinate systems
  • List known quantities and unknowns
  • Check units throughout your calculation
  • Verify limiting cases and reasonableness
